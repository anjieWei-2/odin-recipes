Caprese Salad

Elevate your taste buds with the refreshing simplicity of our Caprese Salad. This iconic Italian classic celebrates the harmony of fresh, high-quality ingredients. Ripe, juicy tomatoes mingle with creamy slices of fresh mozzarella, while fragrant basil leaves add a burst of aromatic freshness. A drizzle of extra-virgin olive oil imparts a luscious, fruity note to this masterpiece, and for those seeking an extra layer of flavor, a touch of balsamic vinegar brings a delightful sweetness. With a dash of salt and pepper to balance the flavors, this salad is a celebration of summertime simplicity. Whether as a starter or a side, it's a plate of pure, unadulterated elegance that captures the essence of Mediterranean cuisine in every bite. Savor the taste of sunshine with each forkful of this Caprese Salad.

Ingrediends

  • 4 ripe tomatoes, sliced.
  • 8 ounces (about 1 cup) fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced.
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves.
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil.
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar (optional).
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.

Steps

  1. Arrange the tomato and mozzarella slices on a serving platter, alternating them.
  2. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the tomato and mozzarella slices.
  3. Drizzle extra-virgin olive oil evenly over the tomato and mozzarella slices.
  4. If desired, you can drizzle balsamic vinegar over the salad for extra flavor. This step is optional but adds a delightful tangy sweetness.
  5. Season the salad with a pinch of salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste.
